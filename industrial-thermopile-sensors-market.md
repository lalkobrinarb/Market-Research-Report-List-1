<p><p>The Industrial Thermopile Sensors market primarily comprises two types: Thermopile Infrared Sensors and Thermopile Laser Sensors. Thermopile Infrared Sensors detect temperature variations by measuring infrared radiation, making them ideal for non-contact temperature monitoring in various industrial processes. In contrast, Thermopile Laser Sensors combine thermopile technology with laser emission for high-precision temperature measurements, particularly in challenging environments. Both sensor types enhance efficiency and accuracy in industrial applications, offering robust solutions for thermal management, safety, and process control.</p></p>
