<p><p>The Portable Anaesthesia Machines market can be categorized into high flow and low flow anesthesia machines. High flow anesthesia machines deliver a greater volume of fresh gas to the patient, allowing for rapid induction and maintaining anesthesia effectively, often utilized in procedures requiring swift changes in anesthetic depth. In contrast, low flow anesthesia machines use a reduced fresh gas flow, promoting cost-efficiency and minimizing environmental impact while enhancing patient safety during prolonged procedures. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
