<p><strong>The Medical Implantable Defibrillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators</li><li>Transvenous Implantable Cardioverter‐Defibrillator</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical implantable defibrillator market comprises three main types. Subcutaneous Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators (S-ICDs) are positioned under the skin and deliver shocks to the heart without wires, minimizing infection risks. Transvenous Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillators (T-ICDs) utilize leads inserted into the heart through blood vessels, providing advanced monitoring and therapy capabilities. The "Other" category includes less common devices and innovative technologies that may offer unique functionalities or specific patient solutions, reflecting ongoing advancements in cardiac care.</p></p>
