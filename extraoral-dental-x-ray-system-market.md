<p><ul><li>Panoramic/Cephalometric X-ray Units</li><li>CBCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extraoral dental x-ray system market includes various types such as panoramic/cephalometric x-ray units and Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) systems. Panoramic units capture a single image of the entire dental arch, providing a comprehensive view of teeth, jaw, and other structures, while cephalometric units focus on lateral skull images for orthodontic assessments. CBCT, on the other hand, offers three-dimensional imaging, allowing for enhanced diagnostics and treatment planning, particularly in implantology and complex cases. These technologies improve diagnostic accuracy and patient care in dentistry.</p></p>
