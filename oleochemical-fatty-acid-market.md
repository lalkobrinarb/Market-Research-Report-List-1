<p><p>The oleochemical fatty acid market is primarily categorized into unsaturated and saturated fatty acids. Unsaturated fatty acids, which contain one or more double bonds, are often derived from natural sources like vegetable oils and are used in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals due to their health benefits. In contrast, saturated fatty acids, containing no double bonds, are typically solid at room temperature and are found in animal fats and tropical oils, serving applications in industrial lubricants, soaps, and candles.</p></p>
