<p><p>The COVID-19 rapid diagnostic test market comprises three main types: immunoassays, genomic tests, and rRT-PCR. Immunoassays detect antibodies or antigens, providing quick results for active or past infections. Genomic tests analyze viral RNA to confirm infection, while whole genome sequencing (WGS) offers comprehensive insights into the virus's genetic material, aiding in variant detection. rRT-PCR, a widely used method, is essential for confirming active infections with high sensitivity and specificity, making it a pivotal tool in pandemic management.</p></p>
