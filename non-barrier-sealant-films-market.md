<p><p>The Non-Barrier Sealant Films market is classified into two main types: mono-layer films and multi-layer films. Mono-layer films consist of a single polymer layer, offering straightforward production and cost-efficiency. They are suitable for applications requiring basic sealing properties. In contrast, multi-layer films are composed of multiple layers of different materials, enhancing barrier properties, strength, and versatility. These films cater to demanding applications that require superior protection and longer shelf life, making them ideal for food packaging and medical uses.</p></p>
