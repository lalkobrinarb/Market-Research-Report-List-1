<p><p>The hair color products market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-term color, typically lasting a few washes, ideal for experimentation. Semi-permanent dyes last longer, usually up to 24 shampoos, gradually fading while blending with the natural hair color. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, offering long-lasting color that endures through multiple washes, making them suitable for a complete color change. Each type serves different consumer needs based on longevity and commitment.</p></p>
