<p><strong>The Heat Treatment Temperature Control Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ON/OFF</li><li>PID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat treatment temperature control equipment market can be segmented into two primary types: ON/OFF control and PID control. ON/OFF control operates by toggling the heating source on or off based on threshold temperatures, providing a simple and cost-effective solution but with less precision. In contrast, PID (Proportional-Integral-Derivative) control offers advanced regulation by continuously adjusting the heating elements based on the temperature difference, ensuring tighter control and improved stability, making it suitable for demanding applications in industries like metal processing and manufacturing.</p></p>
