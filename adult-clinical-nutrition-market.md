<p><strong>The Adult Clinical Nutrition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral Nutrition</li><li>Enteral Nutrition</li><li>Parenteral Nutrition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adult clinical nutrition market comprises three main types: oral nutrition, enteral nutrition, and parenteral nutrition. Oral nutrition includes nutritional supplements and fortified foods consumed by patients who can eat but require additional nutrients. Enteral nutrition involves delivering nutrients directly into the gastrointestinal tract via tubes, suitable for those unable to eat orally. Parenteral nutrition provides essential nutrients intravenously, bypassing the digestive system, making it ideal for patients with severe digestive issues or those unable to consume food safely.</p></p>
