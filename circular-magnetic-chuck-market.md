<p><ul><li>Electro-Permanent Chuck</li><li>Permanent Magnet Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circular magnetic chuck market primarily comprises two types: electro-permanent chucks and permanent magnet chucks. Electro-permanent chucks combine the benefits of both permanent and electromagnets, allowing for quick activation and deactivation without continuous power supply, making them ideal for precise applications. In contrast, permanent magnet chucks utilize fixed magnets for consistent holding strength, suited for simpler tasks where power is not a concern. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific machining and clamping requirements in various industries.</p></p>
