<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Elevator</li><li>Traction Elevator</li><li>Climbing Elevator</li><li>Pneumatic Elevator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator modernization market comprises several types of elevators, each with unique characteristics. Hydraulic elevators use fluid-driven pistons for movement and are ideal for mid-rise buildings. Traction elevators rely on cables and pulleys, offering energy efficiency, often used in high-rise structures. Climbing elevators, suited for construction sites, use a special mechanism to ascend vertical surfaces. Pneumatic elevators operate using air pressure, providing a unique, space-saving solution for residential and small buildings, appealing for retrofitting older structures.</p></p>
