<p><p>The next generation centrifuge market includes two primary types: refrigerated centrifuges and ambient centrifuges. Refrigerated centrifuges are designed to maintain low temperatures during sample processing, ensuring the stability of temperature-sensitive materials. These are essential in fields like biotechnology and molecular biology. On the other hand, ambient centrifuges operate at room temperature and are suitable for general centrifugation tasks where temperature control is not critical. Both types cater to diverse applications in research, clinical, and industrial laboratories.</p></p>
