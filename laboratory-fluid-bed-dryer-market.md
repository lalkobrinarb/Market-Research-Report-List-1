<p><p>The Laboratory Fluid Bed Dryer market is divided into two primary types: Single Layer and Multi-Layer dryers. Single Layer dryers are designed for smaller batch sizes and provide efficient air circulation for uniform drying of individual samples. In contrast, Multi-Layer dryers accommodate larger volumes by utilizing several layers of products stacked within the same unit, enhancing throughput while maintaining product quality. Both types are essential for optimizing drying processes in various industries, focusing on efficiency and precision.</p></p>
