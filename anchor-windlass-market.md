<p><strong>The Anchor Windlass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anchor windlass market comprises two main types: horizontal and vertical windlasses. Horizontal windlasses are designed with the drum horizontally aligned, allowing for easier installation and operation, often found on smaller vessels. In contrast, vertical windlasses have a vertically positioned drum, facilitating more compact designs and making them suitable for larger ships with heavier anchors. Both types serve to control anchor deployment and retrieval, critical for safe marine operations, but are chosen based on vessel size and usage.</p></p>
