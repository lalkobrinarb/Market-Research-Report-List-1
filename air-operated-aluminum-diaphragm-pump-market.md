<p><p>The Air Operated Aluminum Diaphragm Pump market is segmented into single acting and double acting types. Single acting pumps utilize one diaphragm for each stroke, allowing fluid to be drawn in and expelled in an efficient cycle, typically suited for lower flow applications. In contrast, double acting pumps use two diaphragms that operate simultaneously, providing a continuous flow with higher efficiency and capacity, making them ideal for demanding industrial processes. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the application.</p></p>
