<p><p>The waveform generator market encompasses various types of signal generators used in electronics and testing applications. Square-wave generators produce square-shaped signals, ideal for digital circuits. Triangle generators create linear waveforms, useful for modulation applications. Sawtooth generators produce ramp-like signals, often used in synths and oscillators. Sine-wave generators create smooth, sinusoidal waves essential for audio applications. Arbitrary function generators can produce complex, user-defined waveforms, catering to specialized testing needs. Other categories may include noise generators and pulse generators, serving niche applications.</p></p>
