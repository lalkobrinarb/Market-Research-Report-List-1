<p><strong>The Single-mode Fiber Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) Technology</li><li>Thin-film Filter Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-mode fiber coupler market features two primary technologies: Fused Biconical Taper (FBT) and Thin-film Filter technology. FBT utilizes the fusion and tapering of two fibers to create a coupler, allowing efficient signal splitting or combining. It is known for its simplicity and low cost. On the other hand, Thin-film Filter technology employs optical coatings on fiber ends to achieve precise wavelength filtering and coupling, offering higher performance for specific applications. Both technologies cater to diverse telecommunications needs.</p></p>
