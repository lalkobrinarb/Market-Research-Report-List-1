<p><p>The solar pumps market comprises two main types: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, pulling water from deep sources such as wells, making them ideal for agricultural and irrigation purposes. Conversely, surface pumps are located above the water source and are used for applications like irrigation and drainage. Both types harness solar energy, reducing reliance on conventional power sources and promoting sustainability in water management, particularly in remote or off-grid areas.</p></p>
