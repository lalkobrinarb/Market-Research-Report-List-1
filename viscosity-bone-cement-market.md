<p><p>The viscosity bone cement market is categorized into three types: low, medium, and high viscosity bone cement. Low viscosity bone cement is fluid and ideal for minimally invasive procedures, allowing for easy application and effective penetration. Medium viscosity offers a balance between flow and stability, suitable for standard orthopedic applications. High viscosity bone cement is thicker and provides enhanced support and fixation, often used in load-bearing situations or complex surgeries. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
