<p><p>The Positive Glue Stripping Liquid market is categorized into two primary types: solvent-based and water-based glue stripping liquids. Solvent-based glue stripping liquids utilize organic solvents for effective adhesive removal, offering quick evaporation and robust performance, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based glue stripping liquids are environmentally friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, and are safer to use with fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them suitable for residential and light commercial applications.</p></p>
