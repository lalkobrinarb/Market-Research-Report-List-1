<p><p>Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) sensors are categorized based on their pressure measurement capabilities, typically expressed in bar. The 1 Bar sensors measure pressure up to approximately 14.5 psi, suitable for low-pressure applications. 2 Bar sensors handle pressures up to around 29 psi, often used in medium-performance vehicles. The 3 Bar sensors support pressures up to approximately 43.5 psi, commonly found in performance-oriented engines. Finally, 4 Bar sensors can measure pressures up to about 58.0 psi, designed for high-performance and turbocharged applications.</p></p>
