<p><p>The Ground Support Equipment (GSE) Tires market features three main types: solid rubber, foam-filled, and air-filled tires. Solid rubber tires offer durability and resistance to punctures, making them ideal for rugged environments. Foam-filled tires combine the benefits of solid and air-filled tires, providing cushioning while eliminating the risk of air loss. Air-filled tires, while offering a smoother ride and better shock absorption, are more susceptible to punctures. Each type caters to specific operational needs in aviation and other industrial applications.</p></p>
