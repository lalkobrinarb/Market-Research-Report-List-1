<p><p>The hernia biologic meshes market is categorized into partially absorbable and fully absorbable types. Partially absorbable meshes provide temporary support, gradually absorbing while allowing tissue integration, making them suitable for specific repair procedures. In contrast, fully absorbable meshes are designed to dissolve completely over time, facilitating natural tissue regeneration without leaving any permanent material. Both types aim to enhance healing and reduce complications, catering to different surgical needs and patient profiles in hernia repair procedures.</p></p>
