<p><p>The physical therapy software market is primarily divided into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a clinic's servers, offering complete control over data and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing accessibility from anywhere, automatic updates, and lower initial costs. These options cater to different needs, with on-premise favored by larger practices and cloud-based solutions appealing to smaller clinics for their flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
