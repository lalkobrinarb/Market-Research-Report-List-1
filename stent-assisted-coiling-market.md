<p><p>Stent-Assisted Coiling is a minimally invasive endovascular procedure used to treat aneurysms in blood vessels, particularly in the brain. It involves placing a stent at the neck of an aneurysm to support the structure while enabling the insertion of coils to effectively occlude the aneurysm. This technique helps prevent aneurysm rupture and promotes the stability of the blood vessel.</p><p>The Stent-Assisted Coiling Market is witnessing significant growth due to advancements in medical technology and an increasing prevalence of aneurysms and cardiovascular diseases. <p><strong>The Stent-Assisted Coiling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Expandable Stents</li><li>Liquid Embolic Devices</li><li>Flow-diversion Devices</li><li>Embolic Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stent-assisted coiling market comprises various devices used in neurovascular interventions. Self-expandable stents are designed to provide structural support to blood vessels during coiling procedures. Liquid embolic devices deliver a liquid polymer to occlude blood flow in target areas. Flow-diversion devices redirect blood flow away from aneurysms, promoting healing. Embolic coils are specifically crafted to fill aneurysms and prevent blood flow. These technologies enhance treatment options, improve patient outcomes, and minimize risks in complex vascular procedures.</p></p>
