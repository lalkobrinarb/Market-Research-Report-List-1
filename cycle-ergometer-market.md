<p><p>The cycle ergometer market is primarily divided into two types: upright cycle ergometers and recumbent cycle ergometers. Upright cycle ergometers mimic traditional bicycles, offering a more intense workout that engages the core and lower body muscles. They are popular in fitness and rehabilitation settings. In contrast, recumbent cycle ergometers feature a more comfortable, reclined position, providing support to the back and reducing strain on joints, making them ideal for users with mobility issues or those seeking a gentler workout experience.</p></p>
