<p><p>The central vacuum hose market comprises three main types: dual voltage, low voltage, and no voltage hoses. Dual voltage hoses can operate both electrically and manually, allowing versatility for various cleaning tasks. Low voltage hoses provide a constant electrical supply to power attachments but maintain a lower voltage level for safety. No voltage hoses, on the other hand, are purely manual and do not incorporate electrical features, making them ideal for simpler, less intensive cleaning applications. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>
