<p><p>Pressure seal piston check valves are critical components in flow control systems, ensuring unidirectional flow while preventing backflow. In the market, two primary types are distinguished by their joining methods. Socket weld type features a welding process that connects pipes at their ends, suitable for small diameters and high pressure applications. Butt welding type, on the other hand, involves welding the pipes end-to-end, providing a stronger and more durable connection, often used for larger diameters and higher stresses in industrial environments.</p></p>
