<p><p>The axial compressors market is divided into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their design and functionality. Single-stage axial compressors consist of a single rotor and stator configuration, ideal for applications requiring moderate pressure increases. Multi-stage axial compressors, on the other hand, feature multiple stages with several rotor and stator pairs, enabling higher pressure ratios and efficiency. These types cater to various industries, including aerospace, power generation, and HVAC systems, depending on the specific requirements for airflow and pressure.</p></p>
