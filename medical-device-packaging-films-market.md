<p><p>The medical device packaging films market encompasses various types, primarily monolayer films and coextruded films. Monolayer films consist of a single layer, offering basic protection and barrier properties, making them cost-effective for certain applications. In contrast, coextruded films are composed of multiple layers, allowing for enhanced performance, such as improved moisture resistance and mechanical strength. These films cater to diverse medical packaging needs, ensuring product safety and extending shelf life while meeting regulatory standards in healthcare settings.</p></p>
