<p><p>The solids handling pumps market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal solids handling pumps. Vertical solids handling pumps are designed for applications where space is limited and are ideal for pumping thick, viscous slurries from deep pits or tanks. In contrast, horizontal solids handling pumps are commonly used for transporting large volumes of slurry over longer distances, providing efficient flow in various industrial applications. Both types are essential for effectively managing solids in wastewater treatment and mining processes.</p></p>
