<p><p>The Neem Seed Oil Extractor Machine market features two primary types: mechanical pressing and solvent extraction. Mechanical pressing utilizes physical force to extract oil from neem seeds, often resulting in a more natural product with minimal chemical residue. In contrast, solvent extraction involves using chemical solvents to enhance oil yield, which can lead to higher efficiency but may introduce residual solvents in the final product. Both methods cater to varying consumer preferences for oil purity and extraction efficiency in the neem oil sector.</p></p>
