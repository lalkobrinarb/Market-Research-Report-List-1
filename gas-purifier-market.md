<p><p>The gas purifier market consists of three main types: single column purifiers, double column purifiers, and multi-column purifiers. Single column purifiers are designed for low-volume applications, providing simple gas purification with minimal complexity. Double column purifiers offer improved efficiency and redundancy, enabling continuous operation through alternating columns. Multi-column purifiers are suitable for high-capacity applications, allowing for more extensive purification processes by utilizing multiple columns in parallel, thereby enhancing throughput and ensuring consistent gas quality across various operational conditions.</p></p>
