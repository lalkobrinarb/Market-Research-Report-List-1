<p><p>The personal dosimeter market primarily includes pen dosimeters and direct read electronic dosimeters. Pen dosimeters are portable, handheld devices used to measure and record radiation exposure over time, often featuring simple, analog displays for convenient reading. In contrast, direct read electronic dosimeters utilize advanced technology to provide immediate readings of radiation exposure, often with digital displays and data logging capabilities. Both types are essential for monitoring radiation safety in workplaces like hospitals, nuclear plants, and research facilities.</p></p>
