<p><p>The H-Bridge driver market consists of Single H-Bridge and Dual H-Bridge types, primarily used for controlling DC motors. A Single H-Bridge can drive one motor in both directions, making it suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, a Dual H-Bridge can control two motors independently, offering more versatility and efficiency in complex systems. These drivers are integral to robotics, automation, and electric vehicles, enabling precise motor control and enhancing performance across various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
